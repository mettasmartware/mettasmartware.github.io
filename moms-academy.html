Moms’ Academy

Mom’s Academy is a handy mobile application for future moms, which provides them with the most important information about pregnancy and baby development.

The app comes with a convenient personal calendar, where a pregnant woman can make notes and set reminders about the upcoming visits to the doctor and other related plans. What’s more, it’s easy to make an online appointment with a doctor here. A woman can track all the changes that are happening to a baby before the birth and after it until the age of 12 years old. There is even a special field, where you can upload the first photo of your baby and specify the birth date, weight and height.
