Mobile Secretary

Mobile Secretary is an application that will be handy to all businessmen, who have lots of plans and meetings and are afraid to overlook them.

It makes it possible to set different tasks and control their fulfillment. A business owner can store information about other company workers and get quick access to their database. There is a handy task planner and a convenient calendar to track all the appointments. This is a must-have tool for every business owner.
