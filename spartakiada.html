Spartakiada

This is a nice mobile app for all the sports fans, who wish to stay informed about all the news and events happening in the world of sports.

It does not only make it possible to create online communities based on the interests of users, but it also encourages people to go in for sports through the competition spirit and rating system. Users can choose their co-thinkers here and chat with them on any sports topics. The app involves over 200 kinds of sports and it’s completely free.
