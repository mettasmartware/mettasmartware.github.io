FishApp

FishApp is a social network for fishermen that makes it possible for them to find their co-thinkers.

It’s also possible to plan the upcoming trips and publish intriguing fishing photo reviews here. Each user can register with the social account via any mobile device, upload photos and personal info, make friends, add news and create events specifying their date, time and location on the map. It’s very convenient and handy for all fishing fans.
