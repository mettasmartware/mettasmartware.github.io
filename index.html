Stages of App Development

1. Project Idea and Consultation

People come to our company to implement their ideas into life and automatize business processes. Our web developers and analysts will provide you with a free consultation.

2. Project Estimation and Proposition

A web developer makes project evaluation to provide a client with a preliminary commercial offer regarding the web application development.

3. Backlog and Contract

This stage implies meeting with a product owner to discuss the details of a mobile app. A web developer prepares a project backlog in cooperation with a client. Right after that, the contract is signed.

4. Sprint Planning

On this stage, we meet with a client to agree on the tasks that will be completed at the upcoming sprint.

5. Retrospective Review

Demonstration of the work results for a week. The team analyses the risks and problems, completes the sprint, optimizes the further process of app creation.

6. The Mobile App Is Ready

A client gets the top notch readymade application that can further be used to meet specific business needs and requirements.
