Wedding Dress Complex

Wedding Dress is a mobile app that makes it possible to select wedding dresses, sorting them out by the major parameters you consider important.

The application contains an impressive catalogue of dresses one may choose from. The catalogue is regularly updated, which makes the choice almost unlimited. A handy tool for a contemporary bride!
